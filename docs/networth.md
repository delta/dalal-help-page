# How a transaction happens

1. Once you place a order in Dalal Street, its added to the ```order book```.

> **Order Book** is a list containing all the orders.

2. Relevant stocks and cash is moved to Reserved Stocks and Cash. 
 - If you place a ```Bid Order``` for 5,000. That money is moved from your cash in hand to Reserved Cash.
 - If you are trying to sell 10 Stocks of Dalal, those stocks are moved from ```Stocks in Hand``` to ```Reserved Stocks``` 

**Note**: Reserved stocks and cash are included in your net worth calculation. It will only be deducted once the order is filled.
